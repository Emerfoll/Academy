How I got here

I started working warehouse jobs at the age of 18 and in 2017 I decided to persue a job for which I had more interest in. After a bit of searching, I came across eHome LLC. Working on computers all day and helping customers with technical issues is exactly what I wanted in a job; it has been a great opportunity to learn everything I know about Macs and even become an Apple Certified Mac Technician. However I've found myself wanting more out of my work.

I decided to start looking into programming to see where that would lead me. It was then that a friend asked if I had heard of Prime Digital Academy. I was cautiously curious and thought "what the heck, I might as well apply." It seemed to have pretty comprehensive questions, nothing too unexpected, until I got to the technical section. "We would like you to create an HTML version of your resume." As someone who has never even considered web development, this seemed like a daunting task. "Where do I even start?" I wondered. Well it turns out the best way to start it is to just dive right in. Since discovering Prime a little over 3 weeks ago, I have watched many hours of videos on HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and a few others and I have found my new passion.

Creating a website version of my resume has been one of the most fulfilling challenges any application has inspired me to do and I hope all the work I have put into my site doesn't go unnoticed. I look forward to working with you during the full stack engineering course and showing you that I am truly capable of exceeding expectations. Thank you for this opportunity and opening the door to unlocking a new passion.

Sincerely,

Chris Emerfoll
