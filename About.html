Working towards a new career

I started working warehouse jobs at the age of 18 and in 2017 I decided to persue a job for which I had more interest in. After a bit of searching, I came across my job as an Apple computer technician. Working on computers all day and helping customers with technical issues is exactly what I wanted in a job; it has been a great opportunity to learn everything I know about Macs and even become an Apple Certified Mac Technician. However I've found myself wanting more out of my work.

I decided to start looking into programming to see where that would lead me. Through a series of events I came to learn about Prime Digital Academy. I was cautiously curious and thought "what the heck, I might as well apply." It seemed to have pretty comprehensive questions, nothing too unexpected, until I got to the technical section. "We would like you to create an HTML version of your resume." As someone who has never even considered web development, this seemed like a daunting task. "Where do I even start?" I wondered. Well it turns out the best way to start it is to just dive right in. Since discovering Prime a little over 3 weeks ago, I have watched many hours of videos on HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and a few others and I have found my new passion, Web Development.

Creating a website version of my resume has been one of the most fulfilling challenges any application has inspired me to do and now I need help to pursue my new passion. I have never been someone to ask for money, but because of some past mistakes I do not have the credit to take out a loan and the cost of the class ($16k) is more than I can currently afford on my own. If you are in a position to donate I truly appreciate any and all help you can give.

Sincerely,

Chris Emerfoll
